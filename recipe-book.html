What I Learned

Through this project, I gained a solid understanding of how to set up and work with Express as a backend server framework, which was essential in handling routes and managing HTTP requests. Using body-parser made processing incoming data seamless, allowing me to easily extract and work with information sent from the frontend.

Managing environment variables with dotenv taught me the importance of keeping sensitive information secure and out of my main codebase, a best practice I will carry forward. I also discovered how uuid simplifies the creation of unique identifiers for recipes, helping maintain data integrity across the app.

Additionally, integrating nodemon significantly improved my development workflow by automatically restarting the server whenever I saved changes, saving me valuable time during testing and debugging.

All these experiences combined have greatly enhanced my backend development skills and prepared me to tackle more complex full-stack projects with confidence.
